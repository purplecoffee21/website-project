The Chess Hotel

Our chess hotel is themed around the game, a list of features for our rooms includes:

  • 64 rooms on 8 floors, 8 on ean floors
  • Chess themed wallpaper
  • Kitchen and bathroom taps shaped like chess pieces
  • The rooms are black and white
  • Rooms are labeled the same as squares on a chess board

The Game

When you enter any room, you end up in a small room, with 2 other doors, one which only your key will work (which is your personal space), the other door, which anyone can access will (not always) contain a chess piece that can be moved to another room (as a move in this game) When you first everyone who comes is assigned a side so you can only move a piece of your own colour, and need to keep in contact with people on the same team. On the outide will be a video image of what piece (if any) is in that room.
